About Us

B.L.D.E.A’s V.P. Dr. P.G. Halakatti College of Engineering and Technology was established in 1980 by Bijapur Lingayath District Education Association. The institution offers 9 undergraduate programs and research programs in two disciplines.

Departments

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Information Science & Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
  • Computer Science (AI & ML)
  • Computer Science (Data Science)
